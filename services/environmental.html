Environmental Engineering

Budinger & Associates, Inc. has provided environmental services since 1988. In that time, we have completed hundreds of environmental projects. Our geotechnical experience enhances our environmental engineering capabilities as we are very familiar with the environmental effective determination of subsurface characteristics, which is fundamental to both fields. Our services include:

  • Site assessment, design and remediation
  • Underground storage tank removal
  • Landfill siting
  • Wastewater management and groundwater remediation
  • Regulatory compliance and permitting with -
    • Washington State Department of Ecology
    • Idaho Department of Environmental Quality
    • Army Corps of Engineers regulations

The majority of our environmental projects are for facilities such as:

  • Industrial
  • Commercial
  • Superfund
  • Automotive
